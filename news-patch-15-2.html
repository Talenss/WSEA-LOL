The highly anticipated Patch 15.2 is now live across all regions, bringing significant balance changes to 15 champions and introducing new items that will reshape the meta. Our balance team has been carefully monitoring the competitive scene and solo queue to ensure these changes create a healthier game environment.

TL;DR: Mid-lane mages get scaling buffs, ADCs receive early game power, jungle items reworked, and 3 items get price adjustments.

The mid-lane has been dominated by assassins and control mages for too long. We're giving burst mages the tools they need to compete in the early and mid game while maintaining their late-game scaling fantasy.
