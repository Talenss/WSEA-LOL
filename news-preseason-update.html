üìñ Rune Tree Updates

All five rune trees (Precision, Domination, Sorcery, Resolve, Inspiration) have received updates. New keystones offer fresh playstyles while classic runes get stat adjustments.

  • Precision: New keystone for attackers. Grants scaling attack damage based on critical strikes
  • Domination: Reworked damage keystones. More options for aggressive junglers and assassins
  • Sorcery: New haste-focused keystone. Better for ability haste scaling
  • Resolve: Reworked defensive runes. Better scaling for tanky supports and top laners
  • Inspiration: New utility keystone. Provides flexible bonuses for support roles

üéØ Late Game Changes

We're making adjustments to ensure late game is more decisive and less drawn out. Games that reach 35+ minutes will have clearer win conditions.

Baron Buff Changes: Enhanced stats for 2 minutes after Baron kill. Makes closing games more manageable.

Tower Defenses: Late game towers gain damage bonus when multiple enemies approach. Encourages better positioning.

Inhibitor Changes: Respawn timers adjusted. Destroyed inhibitors respawn after 5 minutes (up from 4).

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Mechanics

Several fundamental combat mechanics have been adjusted for better gameplay:

  • Crowd Control: Stun duration capped at 2.5 seconds (was 3 seconds)
  • Damage Spikes: Burst damage reduced slightly across the board. Encourages teamwork
  • Healing: Grievous Wounds now reduces healing by 40% (was 35%)
  • Shields: Maximum shield values adjusted for better balance

üéÆ What to Expect in Preseason

Preseason is the perfect time to experiment with new builds and strategies. Here's what the community should expect:

Chaos Phase (Week 1-2): Everyone experimenting with new items and runes. Expect unusual builds and playstyles.

Meta Settling (Week 3-4): Competitive players find optimal builds. Meta begins to solidify.

Balance Patches: Rapid hotfixes as balance issues are discovered. Be prepared for adjustments.

üìä Performance Tuning

We've optimized game performance for preseason. New items and effects should run smoothly even in late-game fights. FPS improvements especially for team fights with many visual effects.
